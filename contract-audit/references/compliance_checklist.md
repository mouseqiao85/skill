# Contract Compliance Checklist

## Federal Law Compliance

### Antitrust Considerations
- [ ] No price-fixing arrangements
- [ ] No market allocation agreements
- [ ] No boycotts or group refusals to deal
- [ ] No exclusive dealing arrangements that substantially lessen competition
- [ ] No tying arrangements that force purchase of unwanted products

### Securities Law
- [ ] Proper disclosure of material facts
- [ ] No misleading statements about investment opportunities
- [ ] Compliance with registration requirements if applicable
- [ ] Proper handling of insider trading considerations

### Labor and Employment Law
- [ ] Compliance with minimum wage requirements
- [ ] Proper classification of employees vs. independent contractors
- [ ] Non-discrimination clauses
- [ ] Workers' compensation coverage
- [ ] Compliance with overtime regulations

### Consumer Protection
- [ ] Clear and conspicuous disclosures
- [ ] No deceptive trade practices
- [ ] Truth in advertising compliance
- [ ] Proper handling of consumer data
- [ ] Right to cancel/return policies where required

### Privacy and Data Protection
- [ ] GDPR compliance if processing EU citizen data
- [ ] CCPA compliance if processing California resident data
- [ ] HIPAA compliance if handling health information
- [ ] COPPA compliance if collecting children's data
- [ ] Proper data breach notification procedures
- [ ] Adequate data security measures

## State Law Compliance

### General Contract Law
- [ ] Capacity to contract (parties legally able to enter agreement)
- [ ] Legality of purpose (contract not for illegal activity)
- [ ] Consideration present (mutual exchange of value)
- [ ] Proper form requirements met (writing, notarization, witnesses)
- [ ] Statute of frauds compliance for required contract types

### State-Specific Requirements
- [ ] Professional licensing requirements met
- [ ] State-specific disclosure requirements
- [ ] Usury law compliance (interest rate limits)
- [ ] State-specific consumer protection provisions
- [ ] Required contract language (bilingual in some states)

## International Compliance

### Export Controls
- [ ] ITAR compliance for defense articles
- [ ] EAR compliance for dual-use items
- [ ] OFAC sanctions screening
- [ ] Foreign national access restrictions

### Import Regulations
- [ ] Customs compliance
- [ ] Country of origin labeling
- [ ] Anti-dumping duties
- [ ] Countervailing duties

### International Trade
- [ ] Compliance with trade agreements
- [ ] Transfer pricing considerations
- [ ] Permanent establishment risks
- [ ] Withholding tax obligations

## Industry-Specific Compliance

### Financial Services
- [ ] Banking regulation compliance
- [ ] Investment advisor regulations
- [ ] Insurance regulation compliance
- [ ] Money transmission licensing
- [ ] Anti-money laundering requirements
- [ ] Bank Secrecy Act compliance

### Healthcare
- [ ] FDA approval/authorization for medical devices/drugs
- [ ] Clinical trial requirements
- [ ] Medical device reporting obligations
- [ ] Quality system regulations
- [ ] Clinical laboratory improvement amendments (CLIA)

### Environmental
- [ ] EPA regulation compliance
- [ ] OSHA safety requirements
- [ ] Hazardous waste disposal
- [ ] Environmental impact assessments
- [ ] Clean Air Act compliance
- [ ] Clean Water Act compliance

### Telecommunications
- [ ] FCC licensing requirements
- [ ] Universal service fund contributions
- [ ] Truth-in-Billing compliance
- [ ] Customer proprietary network information (CPNI) protection
- [ ] Accessibility requirements (Section 508)

## Documentation and Recordkeeping

### Required Records
- [ ] Contract execution records
- [ ] Amendment and modification history
- [ ] Performance tracking documentation
- [ ] Correspondence files
- [ ] Invoice and payment records
- [ ] Dispute resolution documentation

### Retention Requirements
- [ ] Primary contract documents (typically 7+ years)
- [ ] Financial records (varies by jurisdiction)
- [ ] Tax-related documents
- [ ] Litigation-related materials
- [ ] Regulatory filings and responses

## Risk Assessment Factors

### High-Risk Elements
- [ ] Novel or untested legal structures
- [ ] International operations in restricted countries
- [ ] Regulated industry participation
- [ ] Government contracting
- [ ] Substantial financial commitments
- [ ] Long-term obligations

### Mitigation Strategies
- [ ] Regular compliance audits
- [ ] Legal counsel review for complex matters
- [ ] Insurance coverage for compliance risks
- [ ] Employee training programs
- [ ] Updated policies and procedures
- [ ] Monitoring of regulatory changes

## Post-Execution Compliance

### Ongoing Obligations
- [ ] Filing requirements with government agencies
- [ ] Periodic reporting to regulators
- [ ] Compliance certification maintenance
- [ ] License renewal processes
- [ ] Notification of material changes
- [ ] Annual compliance reviews

### Monitoring Requirements
- [ ] Performance tracking against benchmarks
- [ ] Compliance with ongoing covenants
- [ ] Regular assessment of changing regulations
- [ ] Audit rights and procedures
- [ ] Corrective action protocols
- [ ] Escalation procedures for violations
